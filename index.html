Alejandra Paz Rivera

PhD Candidate in Economics · Universitat de Barcelona

I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at the Universitat de Barcelona. My research sits at the intersection of Health, Public, and Environmental Economics, with a focus on applied micro and policy evaluation. My job market paper studies the impact of extreme heat during pregnancy on birth outcomes in Spain, combining administrative birth records with high-resolution temperature data. I have extensive teaching experience and currently collaborate as a Quantitative Consultant at 3ie.

Research interests: Policy Evaluation · Health · Public & Environmental Economics · Applied Microeconomics
